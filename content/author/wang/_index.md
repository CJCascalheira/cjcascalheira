Shuo "Coco" Wang is a first-year doctoral student in the Counseling Psychology Ph.D. program at New Mexico State University. Her research interests focus on Asian and Asian American sexual minority individuals' mental health issues, self-identity/self-concept development, and practical application and efficiency of therapy. 

Clinically, she enjoys providing bilingual services (English and Mandarin) to new immigrants, sexual minority individuals and other marginalized populations with ACT, EFT and RCT. She is currently working as a research assistant and a practicum counselor. Please feel free to [email her](mailto:shuow3@nmsu.edu) if you have any questions.
