Here is how you can participate in #MpactTDOV, a community campaign to support transgender people in Las Cruces.

Simply create a Facebook profile photo using one of our photo frame filters, designed by the one and only, Bri Nicole.

Step 1

First, go to our discussion page.

Scroll down until you find a Facebook frame that you like, such as being trans and proud, a proud ally parent, or a proud ally mom.

When you find one that you like, click the Try It button.

Step 2

Next, make any adjusts to your photo.

When you are satisfied, click Use as Profile Photo.

Step 3

Return to your profile page and edit your profile photo.

Add #MpactTDOV to the photo description and say that you are at Mpact of Las Cruces.

Celebrate!

Thank you for helping Mpact of Las Cruces to create a safe, supportive space for transgender equality.

If your profile profile photo looks this this, then we can easily find and share your support on the national Transgender Day of Visiblity.
